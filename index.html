About Our Lab

本课题组专注于激光驱动的粒子加速与辐射源研究，致力于开发基于先进靶材（如空心等离子体纤维、纳米结构靶）的新一代加速器技术。我们的研究涵盖激光尾波场电子加速、靶后鞘层质子加速、以及基于激光等离子体的X射线与离子源开发。

Our group focuses on laser-driven particle acceleration and radiation sources, dedicated to developing next-generation accelerator technologies based on advanced targets (such as hollow plasma fibers and nanostructured targets). Research covers laser wakefield electron acceleration, target normal sheath acceleration (TNSA) of protons, and laser-plasma based X-ray and ion sources.

  • 激光加速物理与关键技术
  • 先进微纳结构靶材设计与制备
  • 基于激光等离子体的紧凑中子源与X射线源
  • 激光质子束在放射治疗中的应用
  • 高场强物理与激光核物理
  • Laser Acceleration Physics and Technologies
  • Advanced micro/nano-structured target design
  • Compact neutron and X-ray sources
  • Clinical application of laser-driven protons
  • High-field physics & laser nuclear physics

研究方向

Research Areas

🔥 激光驱动的质子加速

🔥 Laser-Driven Proton Acceleration

利用拍瓦级激光与固体靶相互作用，通过靶后鞘层加速机制产生高能质子束，探索其在肿瘤放疗中的应用潜力。

Using petawatt lasers interacting with solid targets to generate high-energy proton beams via TNSA mechanism, exploring applications in cancer radiotherapy.

🎯 先进靶材与微纳结构

🎯 Advanced Targetry

开发空心等离子体纤维(HPF)、纳米线阵列等新型靶材结构，优化能量耦合效率与束流品质，实现能谱调控与高通量输出。

Developing novel target structures including Hollow Plasma Fibers (HPF) and nanowire arrays to optimize energy coupling efficiency and beam quality.

⚡ 级联加速与束流操控

⚡ Staged Acceleration

设计多阶段加速方案，结合气体与固体靶的优势，突破单级加速的能量极限，构建紧凑型TeV级加速器原型。

Designing multi-stage acceleration schemes combining gas and solid targets to overcome energy limits of single-stage acceleration.

🔬 辐射诊断与束流表征

🔬 Beam Diagnostics

发展基于 Thomson 谱仪、荧光屏与在线监测系统的综合诊断平台，实现激光加速束流的高分辨率能谱与空间分布测量。

Developing comprehensive diagnostic platforms based on Thomson spectrometers and online monitoring systems for high-resolution characterization.
